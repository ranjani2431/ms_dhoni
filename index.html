Mahendra Singh Dhoni

Early Life

Dhoni was born to Pan Singh and Devaki Devi in a Hindu Rajput family. His parents originally hailed from Lwali village in Uttarakhand. Dhoni's family spells their surname as "Dhauni," but due to a spelling mistake in his school certificates, it was recorded as "Dhoni."

Cricket Career

  • Debut: Dhoni made his first-class debut for Bihar in 1999 and his international debut for India in an ODI against Bangladesh on December 23, 2004.
  • Captaincy: He became the captain of the Indian team in 2007 and led the team to victory in several international tournaments, including:
    • 2007 ICC World Twenty20
    • 2011 Cricket World Cup
    • 2013 ICC Champions Trophy
  • Career Statistics: Dhoni has scored 17,266 runs in international cricket, including 10,000+ runs in ODIs. He has taken 634 catches and effected 195 stumpings.

Records and Achievements

  • Tests: Most runs by an Indian wicket-keeper (4,876), most sixes by an Indian captain (51), most dismissals by an Indian wicket-keeper (294).
  • ODIs: Most wins by an Indian captain (110), second-most runs as captain (6,641), first player to score 10,000+ runs with an average over 50.
  • T20Is: Second-most matches as captain (72).

Awards and Honors

  • Padma Bhushan (2018): India's third-highest civilian award.
  • Padma Shri (2009): India's fourth-highest civilian award.
  • Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award (2008): Highest sports honor in India.
  • ICC Cricket Hall of Fame (2025): Inducted for outstanding contributions to cricket.

Personal Life

Dhoni married Sakshi Singh Rawat on 4 July 2010 in Dehradun. Dhoni and his wife have a daughter, Ziva Dhoni who was born on 6 February 2015. He lives in his farmhouse outside Ranchi.Dhoni is an automotive enthusiast and owns a number of bikes and cars in his collection.
